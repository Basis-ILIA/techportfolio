Mediaocean

Program-level overview of the Mediaocean partnership and its three active workstreams.

About Mediaocean

Mediaocean is a strategic partnership covering multiple integration points between Basis and the Mediaocean platform family. The program is currently structured as three workstreams, each with its own scope, owner, and timeline. Use the sidebar to drill into any individual workstream for detailed status, decisions, and open actions.

Workstreams
1
Prisma Integration
Automated two-way ERP integration between Basis and Prisma. Synchronizes campaign planning, IO management, placement-level delivery data, and billing. Currently in solution definition + PoC planning; pilot customer search active.
Owner: Aubrey Lehrmann · PMO: Ilia Fonariov  ·  Target GA: Q1 2027
On Track Pilot Required
2
Innovid Ad Server Integration
Integration between Basis and the Innovid ad server for campaign and placement sync plus delivery reporting at CM360 parity. Currently at risk due to outstanding field gap validation and auth work at Innovid. File-based delivery confirmed as the architecture; engineering target September 1; MCA go-live November 1.
Owner: Jon Iturralde · PMO: Muzzammil Singdi  ·  Eng. Complete: Sep 1, 2026
At Risk Escalating
3
SAM (Social Ads Manager)
Migration of MCA-managed social advertising campaigns to the Basis platform. Meta Ad Scheduling delivery targeted June 6–15, which unlocks 30–40% of $70–80M in annual Meta-directed social spend. Campaign migration begins H2 2026; 50% of customers transitioned by EOY 2026.
Owner: Tim Smith · PMO: Ilia Fonariov  ·  50% Transition Target: EOY 2026
In Progress On Track
