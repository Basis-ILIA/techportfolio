Goal
Transition MCA Social Spend to Basis Platform

Migrate MCA-managed social advertising campaigns from external tools to the Basis platform (Social Ads Manager). Target: 50% of customers transitioned by end of 2026. Once Meta Ad Scheduling is live (June 6–15), an estimated 30–40% of the $70–80M in annual social spend directed to Meta properties becomes available to migrate to SAM.

The social team is appropriately cautious given makegood risk — Innovid's position is that the platform works well enough to start now, but Basis is absorbing the cost of SAM and cannot absorb increased makegoods on top of that. Campaign migration is expected to begin H2 2026 once testing and feature delivery are confirmed.

Key Context
Makegood Risk
The social team is exercising appropriate caution. Basis is absorbing the cost of SAM and cannot simultaneously absorb increased makegoods if campaigns under-deliver on the new platform. Migration will not begin until testing and feature delivery are confirmed. Meta Ad Scheduling (June 6–15) is the key unlock.
Meta Spend Opportunity
Once Meta Ad Scheduling is live, an estimated 30–40% of the $70–80M in annual social spend going to Meta properties becomes available to migrate to SAM. This is the largest single unlock in the near-term migration path.
