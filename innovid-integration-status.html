Full Ad Server Integration -- Campaign, Placement, and Delivery Data

Integrate Basis with the Innovid ad server to enable programmatic synchronization of campaign and placement data and delivery reporting at CM360 parity. The initial first customer is MCA (Boundless). Engineering completion target is September 1, 2026; MCA go-live target is November 1, 2026.

Architecture Context
File-Based Delivery Pivot
The original reporting path ran through Improvado, contracted to connect Basis to Innovid's Reporting API. By March 20, Basis had identified that the Reporting API lacked the CM360 parity fields required. Deeper investigation surfaced additional gaps -- incomplete entity coverage and missing metrics -- raised on April 14. At that point Innovid confirmed no additional API changes would be made and shifted to investigating file-based delivery. Sample files were not received until May 14, nearly two months after gaps were first raised. File-based delivery is now the confirmed working path, but Innovid has not yet provided final confirmation that all required fields will be available in the output.
Authentication -- Static-Token Workaround
Innovid confirmed OAuth is out of scope and would require work on their side. This placed approximately four weeks of additional engineering on Basis to build a static-token workaround. The authentication endpoint was not delivered until April 24. Authentication questions remain open and could introduce further delays.
Basis Position on Disputed Points
Innovid's formal response to Basis's delivery gaps report attributed the file-based pivot to Basis's late engagement of its Data Engineering team, disputed that API limitations caused delays, and framed bulk access gaps as an "enhancement" rather than a missing capability. Basis's position stands: CM360 parity requirements were stated since project inception in February; the file-based pivot reflects Innovid's incomplete API capabilities, not a Basis scope change. A consolidated request-by-request log (all asks, dates, resolution status) is being prepared by PMO for Tim Smith's escalation call.
Escalation
Escalation Call with Innovid Leadership -- Scheduled
A call with Innovid leadership is scheduled for the week of May 26 (Friday May 29). Purpose: bring Innovid leadership up to speed on continued delays, impact on Basis's timeline, and the need for greater urgency. Key points: engineering team responsiveness, absence coverage gaps, and the pattern of slow turnarounds relative to Basis's same-day feedback cadence. This is part of preparation for a commercial renegotiation conversation with Mediaocean leadership.
Confidence Note

Timeline confidence is low. The September 1 engineering target and November 1 MCA go-live assume near-ideal execution with no bugs or back-and-forth delays -- a condition that has not characterized this engagement to date. Innovid's key engineering lead was absent from the May 21 call with no delegate stepping in. Mediaocean leadership attendance on biweekly check-ins has declined since Ramsey's departure. Both sides are experiencing frustration; the goal of the escalation call is to get fact-based and move forward constructively.
